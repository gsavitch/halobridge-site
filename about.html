About HALOBridge.ai

HALOBridge.ai is more than a platform — it’s a philosophy. We believe that intelligence and action belong together. In an era overwhelmed by data, disconnected systems, and manual workflows, we unite the chaos into clarity.

Our Vision

HALO (HealthCare AI Logic Orchestrator) is the foundation of our ecosystem — a zero-trust, locally run AI stack that powers:

  • Gallo AI – Document intelligence + structured data harmonization
  • Doodledoo – Natural language interface across documents + SQL
  • Nightingale – FHIR-ready medical record processing with full provenance
  • GalloDoc – Unified schema for healthcare, finance, legal, and more

Why Local-First?

We built HALOBridge from the ground up to run in secure environments. No cloud dependencies, no vendor lock-in. From regulated industries to private research labs — we protect your data, while extracting its full potential.

Meet the Maker

Founded and engineered by The Curious Moose, HALOBridge is a product of deep industry knowledge and relentless curiosity. What began as a set of scripts to tame document chaos has grown into an enterprise-grade engine for AI-assisted transformation.

Platform Components

  • HALO Core: The orchestrator layer managing model pipelines, logging, and routing in air-gapped systems.
  • Rooster AI: Universal ingestion engine for documents of all kinds, from contracts to shipping labels.
  • Nightingale: Medical record processor that understands FHIR, EHR, and clinical nuance.
  • Doodledoo: The search bar that answers back — hybrid AI search for documents and databases.
  • GalloDoc: Schema and export framework that bridges LLMs, humans, and enterprise systems.

Each tool is modular, secure, and extensible — designed to deliver clarity from chaos at any scale.
